The Craft Layer

June 2026

There is a layer of software work that rarely fits into the pitch but determines whether the product feels real: naming, empty states, latency, recovery, data shape, and the small interface decisions that make a tool understandable.

I like this layer because it is where ideas stop being abstract. The details expose whether a team actually understands the thing it is trying to build.

Good craft is not decoration. It is the discipline of making the system easier to reason about for the next person who touches it.
