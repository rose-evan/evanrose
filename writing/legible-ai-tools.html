Legible AI Tools

June 2026

AI tools become useful when people can see what happened, what changed, and what still needs judgment. The interface should not pretend that uncertainty disappeared just because a model returned an answer.

The best tools give users handles: diff views, state, provenance, constraints, and escape hatches. They make collaboration feel practical instead of magical.

I am interested in the layer where capability becomes usable: the product decisions that help people build faster without losing the thread of their own work.
